Projects

  • Survey director of the evaluation of the Colorado Health Foundations Team Based Care Initiative
  • Lead analyst on the qualitative investigation of the barriers to physical activity in Colorado youth
  • Data collection lead for the evaluation of USAID’s Student Dropout Prevention Pilot Program in Cambodia and Timor-Leste
